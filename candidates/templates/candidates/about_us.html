About Us

Champaran Consultancy Services (CCS)

Champaran Consultancy Services (CCS) is committed to providing free job consultancy services across India. Our mission is to assist unemployed individuals in finding private sector jobs with salaries ranging from ₹8,000 to ₹15,000 per month, based on their qualifications, without charging any fees.

Additionally, we collaborate with industries and business owners to fulfill their hiring needs by connecting them with skilled or fresher candidates.

Currently, CCS is available on Facebook, and soon, our website will be live to ensure seamless job assistance and hiring solutions.
